Design and redesign

"Landing page" website

A One-page website (Landing Page) - this is how promo sites for presenting goods, services, and organizing instant sales are called. We know how to create web resources that are fully prepared for an influx of visitors. A single page will bring huge profits - it can't be any other way.

Website "Catalog"

A website catalog is a full-fledged web resource where consumers can familiarize themselves with the products or services of a company. As a convenient sales tool, a website catalog can increase the profitability of a business and simplify the task of providing the product range to the target audience.

Website “Corp.website”

A corporate website is a large portal that is created not only for communication between an organization and its clients, but also for exchanging information, thoughts, and ideas among employees and management. This electronic resource serves as the face of the company, a sort of "uniform" that evokes respect and interest from partners and contractors.

"Portfolio" Website

A brochure website is a compact online resource, perfect for businesses starting out on the internet. The website typically includes several pages, featuring information about the company, its products and services, a price list, and contact details and coordinates for communication.

"Individual" Website

We are ready to listen to your idea and carry out the task. The cost of the project will depend on the technical task and deadlines for completion.

"Internet store" website

An online store is a website where users can browse and purchase goods or services online. It allows customers to place orders, make payments, and receive delivery directly to their homes. Such a store operates 24/7, making shopping convenient and accessible at any time.

In my work, I adhere to principles

Usability (Ease of use)

Design must be intuitively understandable and simple for the user.

Adaptability and Responsiveness

Websites should be displayed correctly on different devices and screens.

Principles of UX/UI (User Experience and Interface)

Creating interfaces that simplify interaction with the website.

Aesthetics and Visual Hierarchy

Good design is not only about functionality, but also aesthetics.

Consistency maintenance

All design elements were consistent and repeated throughout the entire website.

Principle KISS (Keep It Simple, Stupid)

Minimalism often contributes to better design as it allows for highlighting the main elements.

Fast Loading and Optimization

Web design should be lightweight in order to not overload the page.

Accessibility

Websites should be accessible for all users.

Testing and Analysis

Design should be tested and feedback collected from users.

Business-oriented

Design must meet the objectives and goals of the business.
